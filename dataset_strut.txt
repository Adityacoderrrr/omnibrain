OmniBrain Dataset Structure
1. Healthcare Dataset 🏥

Purpose

Summarize medical reports
Answer questions from medical documents
Extract diseases, medicines, symptoms
Public Datasets
Dataset	Use
PubMed	Medical research papers
MIMIC-III	Clinical records
MedQA	Medical Question Answering
MedMCQA	Medical MCQs
PMC Open Access	Medical articles

Data Format

{
  "document_id": 1,
  "title": "Diabetes Mellitus",
  "text": "...long medical report...",
  "summary": "...short summary..."
}

Expected Size

10,000–100,000 documents

Applications

Medical chatbot
Disease explanation
Report summarization
2. Legal Dataset ⚖️

Purpose

Summarize court judgments
Explain legal contracts
Answer legal questions

Recommended Datasets

Dataset	Use
IN-Abs	Indian Supreme Court judgment summaries
MILDSum	Multilingual Indian legal judgment summaries
LEDGAR	Contract clause classification

These datasets are commonly used for Indian legal document summarization research.

Format

{
 "case_id":101,
 "document":"Long legal document...",
 "summary":"Short judgment summary"
}
